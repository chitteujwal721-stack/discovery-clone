World-renowned

Our private residential communities deliver modern luxury living in the most spectacular settings. With more than thirty years' experience in land development and real estate and relationships with strong venture partners, Discovery always delivers an unparalleled experience.

Mission

Ansari Sami invites you to explore a world where adventure, comfort, and community come together to offer a lifestyle experience unlike any other. With over 40 exclusive properties, our mission is to elevate the art of living and the joy of belonging amid the most breathtaking landscapes in the world.

Founded by Mike Meldman in 1994, Discovery pairs world-class amenities and features—including championship golf courses, bespoke clubhouses, inspired residences, and our signature Outdoor Pursuits program—with exceptional hospitality.

Vision

For more than three decades, Discovery has developed the most sought-after private residential communities in the world. Between glistening coastal oases, peaceful mountain havens, and idyllic lakeside retreats, each distinctive community seamlessly blends our family-focused values with five-star amenities and incomparable service.

Our vertically integrated development philosophy ensures uncompromising quality at every stage—from construction and design to management and operations.

Values

People are our priority

Our goal is to impact the people and communities we are connected to positively. While developing communities and selling real estate helps us accomplish this goal, we prioritize building relationships, creating opportunities, and sharing enriching experiences.

Respect for people, places, and culture

We learn from everyone we meet and every place we go. We are humble stewards of the land and seek to be valuable members of society. We celebrate authenticity and embrace diversity.

Integrity in everything we do

We lean into challenges and embrace them as opportunities to grow. We strive to be open and honest in everything we do, working in good faith to always find solutions.

Embracing fun in the journey

Life is a series of moments and memories worth filling with as much love, joy, and wonder as possible. We consider creativity, adventure, and play essential to our well-being.
